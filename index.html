Musicals Quiz

Do you concentrate at the start?

This quiz is the revenge of the pit band. Sure, everyone remembers the show stopping songs, but what about the poor musicians sitting under the stage in the dark? Who is even listening at the start of the show when they're playing their hearts out and everyone just wants the cast to get on with it?

Below are a selection of clips of the very starts of a number of different shows. All you have to do is to identify them. That's got to be easy, right? Oh, they're quite short though...
